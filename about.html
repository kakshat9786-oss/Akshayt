Our Story

Founded in 2026, ForecastPro emerged from the recognition that small and medium businesses were being left behind in the data analytics revolution. Traditional BI tools were too expensive, complex, and time-consuming for most organizations.

Our team of data scientists, engineers, and business experts set out to build a platform that combines the power of advanced analytics with the simplicity of modern web applications. Today, ForecastPro serves thousands of businesses worldwide, helping them make better decisions and drive growth through data-driven insights.
